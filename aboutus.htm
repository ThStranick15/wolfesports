About Us

Founded in 2022, Wolf Esports is a New Jersey based team created by a few friends who just want to play games.

Wolf Esports started as a joke among friends. Playing competitive games together online every night they wanted to create an identity to represent on a larger stage.

As a team, our main focus is to provide competitive esports content through tournament play. We look to improve and hone our skills in order to move up and compete on a higher level.
